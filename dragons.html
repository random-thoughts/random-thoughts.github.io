Dragon Pictures and Lore

I have been collecting pictures of Dragons for many years. These are some of my favorites along with a bit information about them. Among the hobbies I have picked up over the years are Leather Crafting and Wood Carving. A book I refer to many times is The Great Book of Dragon Patterns by Lora Irish. I could not find a working web site but I did find her Facebook Page, Lora Irish Blog . Her book has not only patterns of Dragons but some stories about Dragons of Old.
